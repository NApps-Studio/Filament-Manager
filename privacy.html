Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Filament Manager ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Summary: The App is designed to be private by design. We do not use servers to store your sensitive data; everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number). The App does not require you to create an account or sign in to any online service.

2. Local Data Storage & Encryption

To function correctly, the App stores the following data locally on your Android device:

  • Access Tokens: Used for authentication with your 3D printing hardware.
  • UID (Unique Identifier): A technical ID used to identify filament profiles.

Security: This data is protected using hardware-backed encryption (Android Keystore). This means the data is tied to your physical device hardware and is inaccessible to other apps or unauthorized users.

3. Data Transmission

All data remains strictly offline. We do not transmit your Access Tokens or UID to any external servers, cloud services, or third parties. There is no background syncing of your private data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses standard Google Play Services for basic app functionality, which may collect anonymous technical information (like device model or crash reports) to improve app stability. No sensitive filament-related data is shared with these services.

5. Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect or transmit any personal data, it is safe for all users. We do not knowingly target or collect information from children under the age of 13.
