Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Filament Manager ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We value your privacy and the security of your 3D printing infrastructure.

Key Privacy Principle: All sensitive printer credentials stay on your device. We do not operate any external servers to collect or store your printer's access data.

1. Information We Process

To provide management features for your 3D printer, the App processes the following technical credentials:

  • Bambu Lab UID: Used to authenticate and establish a secure MQTT connection with your Bambu Lab printer
  • Bambu Lab Access Token: Used to authenticate and establish a secure MQTT connection with your Bambu Lab printer.

2. Data Storage and Hardware Encryption

All credentials (UID and Access Tokens) are stored exclusively on your local device. We use hardware-backed encryption (Android Keystore system) to ensure that these tokens are protected at the hardware level and are not accessible by other applications.

3. Use of Data (MQTT)

The provided tokens are used solely to communicate with your printer via the MQTT protocol. This communication happens either over your local network or via official Bambu Lab communication channels. None of this data is ever sent to NApps Studio or any third-party servers.

4. No Account Creation

Filament Manager does not require you to create an account. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identification information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses standard Google Play Services for core functionality (such as app updates and basic stability monitoring). No printer-specific credentials or filament data are ever shared with these services.
